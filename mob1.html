Musea

UI/UX Case Study • 2025

Overview

Musea is a music discovery platform designed to help users explore songs based on their mood and emotional state. The app focuses on creating a personalized listening experience by combining intuitive UI with curated recommendations.

Problem

Users often struggle to find music that matches their current mood without spending excessive time searching or browsing. Existing platforms prioritize popularity over emotional relevance, leading to a disconnected and overwhelming experience.

Solution

Musea simplifies music discovery by introducing mood-based navigation and personalized recommendations. Users can quickly select how they feel, and the app generates tailored playlists, reducing decision fatigue and enhancing emotional engagement through a clean, immersive interface.
