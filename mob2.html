Schedulink

UI/UX Case Study • 2025

Overview

Schedulink is a smart scheduling platform designed for students to efficiently manage classes, tasks, and deadlines in one centralized system.

Problem

Students frequently face difficulty organizing their academic schedules across multiple platforms, leading to missed deadlines, poor time management, and increased stress.

Solution

Schedulink provides a unified scheduling experience with intuitive task management, calendar integration, and smart reminders. The platform helps users stay organized, prioritize tasks, and maintain productivity through a structured and user-friendly interface.
